# LLM Wiki

The LLM Wiki is a concept proposed by [[Andrej Karpathy]] in April 2026. It re-imagines the traditional wiki and knowledge base as a "codebase" for an LLM "programmer", with [[Obsidian]] serving as the "IDE".

This approach aims to solve a fundamental problem of traditional [[Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)]] systems, which often struggle with knowledge retention and synthesis, leading to information being "forgotten." By structuring knowledge as an interconnected wiki, the LLM can more effectively build upon and synthesize information.
