# Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)

Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) is an architectural pattern for large language models (LLMs) that enhances their ability to generate responses by first retrieving relevant information from a knowledge base. This helps grounding the LLM's responses in factual data and reduces hallucinations.

[[Andrej Karpathy]]'s [[LLM Wiki]] concept aims to address and solve certain limitations of traditional RAG systems, particularly concerning knowledge retention and synthesis, where information might otherwise be "forgotten."
